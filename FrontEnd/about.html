Our Mission

Every year, approximately 1.92 million tonnes of clothes end up in landfills globally. Fast fashion not only contributes significantly to environmental degradation but also perpetuates unsustainable consumption habits. At Decentralized Thrift Store, we believe in promoting sustainable fashion choices by offering a platform for buying and selling pre-loved clothing items. Our decentralized approach aims to reduce clothing waste and encourage mindful shopping practices.
